The small print:

Prices here are in Euros, but the ticket system handles prices in Danish Kroner (DKK). We use an exchange rate of 1€ = 7.50DKK.

If your company is from Denmark, VAT (Danish: Moms) is added. If you company is registred withing the EU and has a valid VAT number, Danish VAT will not be added, instead "reverse charge" is applied. If you or your company is not registred in the EU, VAT will be added. If your company is outside the EU, no VAT will be added.

We use Flexbillet as our ticket broker. If you or your company is not be able to register and pay by credit card, we have other options available. Please contact registration@gr8conf.org for more information.
